CODING A SELF-PORTRAIT

This self-portrait was made with the programming language p5.js and coded on a software called Processing. It was tedious, testing the coordinates of every shape and vector, but I found it satisfying when I would calculate the coordinates in my head and the results would come out how I expected. I sat down, put on music, and coded. I somewhat enjoy math so I found the process therapeutic and I am happy with my end results. Thank you to Nikki Abril for having code that displayed how you can round the corners of rectangles which I used for the frames of my glasses.

Here is the code
